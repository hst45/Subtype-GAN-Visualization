Subtype GAN:
An Unsupervised Integrate Cancer Subtype Model

Subtype-GAN is a deep adversarial learning approach that can extract the latent features of multi-omics data accurately, and uses consensus clustering and the GMM to identify molecular subtypes. Compared with other state-of-the-art subtyping approaches, Subtype-GAN achieved outstanding performance on the benchmark datasets.

Our Innovation

Multi-omics

At the cellular level, multi-omics data can provide a more comprehensive understanding of the underlying causes of cellular activity than the single omic data.

Multi-input

Subtype-GAN take the difference and connections between different omics data's distribution into consideration during the model fitting process.

Unsupervised

Low-dimensional latent features of multi-omics data can be extracted unsupervisingly with GAN model based on adversarial learning.
